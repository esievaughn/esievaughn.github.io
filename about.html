About Me

From UX to Audiology

I’m a first-year Au.D. student passionate about bringing UX and human-centered design thinking to audiologic care. My years as a product owner focused on UX research taught me how to listen deeply, synthesize data, and design for real people. Now I’m applying that same mindset to hearing science, patient counseling, and evidence-based assessment. I believe that audiology and technology share a core purpose: improving communication and quality of life. My goal is to use research-driven insight, empathy, and modern tools to make hearing care more intuitive, inclusive, and empowering.
